Pet-Related Volunteer Opportunities

Volunteering with pets is a rewarding way to make a positive impact in the lives of animals and the community. Below are some local volunteer opportunities in Fresno where you can help:

  • Fresno Humane Animal Services: Walk dogs, assist with pet adoption events, or provide care to animals awaiting their forever homes. Find out how to volunteer at Fresno Humane.
  • Central California SPCA: Volunteer with dog walking, cat care, and adoption events. Learn more about volunteering at the CCSPCA.
  • Fresno County Animal Services: Help with pet adoption, fostering, and animal care. Volunteer with Fresno County Animal Services.
  • Pawsitive Change Program: Volunteer with this program to assist in rehabilitating animals and incarcerated individuals. Get involved with Pawsitive Change in Fresno.
  • Fresno's Pet Adoption & Rescue Center (F-PARC): Foster, volunteer, or help at adoption events. Volunteer with F-PARC.

Volunteering with animals provides a sense of fulfillment, teaches compassion, and helps create a better future for pets in need.
